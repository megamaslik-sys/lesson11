The Hare and the Tortoise
1

Once Upon a Time...

Once upon a time, there was a very fast hare and a very slow tortoise.
2

The Hare Laughed

The hare always laughed at the tortoise because he was so slow.
3

The Challenge

All the animals were tired of listening to the hare. Then one day the tortoise surprised them all: "Let's have a race tomorrow then!"
4

The Race Day

It was a very sunny day and the hare was very hot. "There's a tree. I can rest under it!"
5

The Winner

While the hare was sleeping, the tortoise passed him and was the winner!

What Lesson Do You Learn From The Story?

"Slow and steady wins the race!" Being consistent and determined is more important than being fast but careless.
